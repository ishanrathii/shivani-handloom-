OUR STORY

Weaving Tradition
Into Global Fashion

Founded by Umesh Rathi, Shivani Handlooms brings together the timeless artistry of Indian weaving traditions with contemporary global fashion aesthetics. With over a decade of experience in Indian handloom sourcing, every collection reflects handcrafted excellence, elegant craftsmanship, and heritage-inspired artistry — curated for boutiques, designers, wholesalers, and international fashion houses.

The Art of the Handloom

Six steps that transform nature's finest cotton into heirloom textiles

01

The Harvest

Pure cotton hand-picked at peak bloom from trusted farms across India's textile heartland.

02

The Dye

Yarns steeped in natural dyes — turmeric, indigo, madder root — colours that deepen with every wash.

03

The Design

Kalamkari patterns traced by hand — each motif a dialogue between artisan and 3,000 years of Indian folk art.

04

The Thread

Ikat threads are bound and dyed before weaving, locking geometric patterns at the fibre level.

05

The Loom

Every metre woven by hand on traditional pit-looms — a rhythm unchanged for over three thousand years.

06

The Fabric

The finished cloth — rich with pattern and texture — curated for global boutiques and discerning designers.

Pochampally Ikat is a traditional handloom textile from Pochampally village in Telangana, India. It uses the double-ikat technique where both warp and weft threads are resist-dyed before weaving, creating distinctive geometric patterns. It received a Geographical Indication (GI) tag in 2004, certifying its authenticity as a product of the Pochampally region. Shivani Handlooms stocks authentic GI-quality Pochampally fabric.

Handloom fabric is woven entirely by hand using traditional wooden looms by skilled artisans, resulting in slight natural variations that give each piece character and authenticity. Power loom fabric is machine-woven at high speed, producing perfectly uniform but less distinctive cloth. Handloom fabric is more breathable, eco-friendly, supports traditional artisans, and commands higher value in boutique and export markets. All Shivani Handlooms products are 100% handloom.
